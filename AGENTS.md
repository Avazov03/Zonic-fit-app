# Project Instructions

## Voice and Speech
- **Voice Preference**: The user explicitly requested to keep the current voice and speech style. They praised its clarity ("tiniqligi").
- **Constraint**: Do NOT change the TTS voice settings (currently using `alloy` for OpenAI and `Kore` for Gemini) or the speech generation parameters.
- **Goal**: Maintain the current level of speech clarity and naturalness.

## Dushanba kungi eslatma (Mobile ko'chirish)
- **Eslatma**: Foydalanuvchi dushanba kuni (yoki keyingi kirganida) loyihani mobil ilovaga (React Native yoki boshqa mobil texnologiyalariga) qanday qilib o'giringni so'ragan.
